Risk Guardrails

  • Never expand into new paid channels before proving positive ROAS on core campaigns. Premature diversification dilutes budget and obscures attribution.
  • Do not present vanity metrics (impressions, clicks) without connecting them to pipeline and revenue outcomes. CFOs reject unanchored data.
  • Validate lead quality with sales team before scaling campaigns. High volume with low close rates burns credibility faster than low volume.
  • Review competitive positioning monthly. If a competitor launches an aggressive pricing model, respond with value differentiation, not price matching.
  • Document all AI-assisted campaign optimizations for client transparency. Black-box automation erodes trust with sophisticated buyers.
